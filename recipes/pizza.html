Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish that consists of a flat, round, leavened dough base, a layer of sauce, and one or more toppings. Toppings can include meat, seafood, vegetables, fruit, cheese, olives, oregano, tomatoes, spices, and herbs. Pizza is often baked in a wood-fired oven at a high temperature.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 450 F
  2. Divide the dough in half.
  3. Roll out the dough into a thin, round shape.
  4. Brush the dough with olive oil.
  5. Prepare the toppings. This can include chopped vegetables, charred meat, shredded cheese, and sauce.
  6. Top the pizza with cheese, then toppings, and finally garnishes like basil or pepper.
  7. Bake the pizza for 15â€“20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden brown. If you're using a pizza stone, you can bake the pizza without letting the dough rise first.
  8. Transfer the pizza to a cardboard disc, cookie sheet, or cutting board.

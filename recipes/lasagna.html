Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat or vegetables, and cheese. The pasta is typically wide, flat, and often ruffled, and the dish is often served with a tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground meats and tomato sause"ragu"
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Cheese, ex "mozarella, Ricotta, Parmesan.
  • seasonings and Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare The SauceLasagna can use a variety of sauces, such as marinara, ragù, or béchamel. The meat sauce is usually the first thing to make because it takes the longest. A bechamel sauce is a white sauce that goes well with many vegetables.
  2. Layer The LasagnaAssemble the lasagna by layering repeating layers of noodles, filling, and sauce. You can use wide, flat noodles. Fillings can include meats, vegetables, or ricotta cheese.
  3. BakePreheat the oven to 375°F, place the lasagna on the bottom rack, and cover with foil. Bake until the noodles are tender but still al dente, about 40–45 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for another 20–25 minutes, or until bubbly and puffed, and golden brown.
